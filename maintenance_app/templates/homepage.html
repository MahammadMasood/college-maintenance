About the Portal

The College Maintenance Portal allows staff to send maintenance requests directly to the principal. The principal reviews each request and can either approve or reject it based on priority and need. Approved requests are forwarded to the Staffs. The portal also keeps track of item prices and expenses, helping in budget management and ensuring a smooth, transparent maintenance process within the college.
